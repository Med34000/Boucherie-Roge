# Prompts Images pour Boucherie Rogé

Voici 3 prompts professionnels (en anglais, car souvent mieux compris par les IA) pour générer des images alignées avec le style de votre boucherie : Tradition, Qualité, Terroir.

## 1. Hero Principal (Accueil) - L'Art du Geste
**Prompt :**
> "Cinematic shot of a master French butcher seen from behind, wearing a pristine white coat and apron, meticulously cutting a large piece of high-quality beef on a worn wooden block. Deep focus on the hands and the meat texture. The background shows a traditional, clean, and warm butchery interior with soft, dramatic lighting highlighting the craftsmanship. 8k resolution, photorealistic, professional food photography style, no face visible."

*Effet recherché :* Mise en valeur du savoir-faire, confiance, sérieux.

## 2. Découverte (À Propos) - L'Ambiance Traditionnelle
**Prompt :**
> "Interior wide shot of a traditional artisanal French butcher shop, empty but inviting. A well-stocked refrigerated glass display case filled with fresh gourmet meat cuts, sausages, and terrines. Warm ambient lighting, rustic wooden elements, chalkboard signs with French text. Clean, premium atmosphere. 8k, architectural digest style photography."

*Effet recherché :* Accueillant, propre, abondance, terroir.

## 3. Détail Produit (Contact/Produits) - La Qualité Brute
**Prompt :**
> "Close-up macro shot of a premium raw ribeye steak on a dark slate plate, garnished with a sprig of fresh rosemary and sea salt flakes. Next to it, an antique butcher knife with a wooden handle. Moody lighting, high contrast, rich textures of the meat marbling. 8k, culinary magazine cover style."

*Effet recherché :* Appétissant, qualité supérieure, brut, authentique.
